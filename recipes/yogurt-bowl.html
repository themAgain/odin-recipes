Yogurt Bowl

Simple Yogurt Bowl

Just a yogurt bowl. Easy to make and good for you.

Ingredients

  • Yogurt
  • Selection of fruits
  • Granola
  1. Take your selection of fruits and make sure they are clean and ready to be eaten.
  2. Cut your fruits into bite sized pieces. Set to side.
  3. Add yogurt to bowl.
  4. Add cut fruits to bowl.
  5. Add granola to bowl.
  6. Grab a spoon and enjoy your yogurt bowl.
